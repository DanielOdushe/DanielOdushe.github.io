My skills

My work helps uncover patterns, inform sustainable solutions, guide environmental management, and support smarter, data-driven decisions in both research and practical applications.

Geoscience

I specialize in environmental geology, hydrogeology, and geophysical data interpretation (Seismic, Resistivity, and well logs, Etc.). I am proficient with tools like Petrel, WINREST, RES2DINV, QGIS and Hampson-Russell (CGG) transforming raw geological data into actionable insights for research and practical applications.

Data Science

I leverage Python and Machine Learning (pandas, NumPy, SciPy, matplotlib, Seaborn) to analyze complex datasets, uncover patterns, and build predictive models. My approach ensures precise, data-driven solutions while automating repetitive tasks for efficiency.

Data Analysis

I am skilled in Excel, Power BI, Tableau, and SQL(PostgreSQL, SQLite, MySQL) to manipulate, clean, organize, and Visualise data. I create perceptive dashboards and visualizations, enabling stakeholders to quickly understand complex information and make informed decisions using insights derived from analysed data.

Programming & Automation

Visualization & Reporting

Saline & Freshwater Zonation - Resistivity, Boreholes & ML

Supply Chain Optimization - Python Modeling & Scenario Analysis

Python models for route optimization, demand forecasting, and inventory simulation. Scenario analysis quantifies potential time and cost savings for logistics planning.

Python-driven spatio-temporal study of Nigerian weather on Feb 21, 2024. Cleaned and visualized temperature, rainfall, and wind patterns using pandas, NumPy, GeoPandas, Matplotlib, geopanda and Seaborn etc.

Global COVID-19 Analysis - SQL Driven Insights
