Geoscience

I specialize in environmental geology, hydrogeology, and geophysical data interpretation (Seismic, Resistivity, and well logs, Etc.). I am proficient with tools like Petrel, WINREST, RES2DINV, QGIS and Hampson-Russell (CGG) transforming raw geological data into actionable insights for research and practical applications.

Data Science

I leverage Python and Machine Learning (pandas, NumPy, SciPy, matplotlib, Seaborn) to analyze complex datasets, uncover patterns, and build predictive models. My approach ensures precise, data-driven solutions while automating repetitive tasks for efficiency.

Data Analysis

I create perceptive dashboards and visualizations, enabling stakeholders to quickly understand complex information and make informed decisions using insights derived from analysed data.

Programming & Automation
